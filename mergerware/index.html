Why the top-right corner is empty

Same field, two axes: how far a vendor reaches into integration & value capture (across) vs. how AI-native it is (up). The well-funded AI-natives and the VDR incumbents are all pinned to the pre-deal left. MergerWare is the only one on the right edge — one AI notch from the open corner.

The open lane

Read the map left-to-right. Competitors crowd the left — sourcing and diligence — which is exactly where AI money is pouring in and margins are thinning. The right side, post-merger integration and value capture, is where 70–90% of deals underdeliver, and it sits almost empty across the field. It also happens to be the stage MergerWare already owns and its founder knows cold. The strategic bet writes itself: take AI to value realization, not to the crowded diligence lane.
