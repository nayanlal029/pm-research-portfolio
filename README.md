# MergerWare — product research

A self-initiated product strategy study of MergerWare's M&A SaaS platform and the competitive landscape around it. Built entirely from public sources as a discussion draft.

**▶ Live site:** https://nayanlal029.github.io/MergerWare_research/ *(resolves once GitHub Pages is enabled — see below)*

## Contents

- **Competitive landscape (interactive)** — [`index.html`](./index.html) → live at https://nayanlal029.github.io/MergerWare_research/. A single-file, offline-capable map of the M&A software market: deal-lifecycle coverage across 11 vendors, AI maturity, and a 2×2 positioning view of where the open lane is. The page also links straight to the PRD below.
- **Product strategy & flagship PRD** — [`MergerWare_Product_Strategy_and_PRD.pdf`](./MergerWare_Product_Strategy_and_PRD.pdf) → live at https://nayanlal029.github.io/MergerWare_research/MergerWare_Product_Strategy_and_PRD.pdf. Where MergerWare could play next, three ranked bets, and a full PRD for the leading one — the "Value Engine" (synergy & value-realization intelligence).

After pushing, go to **Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from a branch" → Branch: `main` / `/ (root)` → Save**. The live URL resolves in about a minute.
