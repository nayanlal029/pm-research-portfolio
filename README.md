# MergerWare — product research

A self-initiated product strategy study of MergerWare's M&A SaaS platform and the competitive landscape around it. Built entirely from public sources as a discussion draft.

## Contents

- **Competitive landscape (interactive)** — [`index.html`](./index.html). A single-file, offline-capable map of the M&A software market: deal-lifecycle coverage across 11 vendors, AI maturity, and a 2×2 positioning view of where the open lane is. Once GitHub Pages is enabled (Settings → Pages → deploy from `main`), it serves live at the repository's Pages URL.
- **Product strategy & flagship PRD** — [`MergerWare_Product_Strategy_and_PRD.pdf`](./MergerWare_Product_Strategy_and_PRD.pdf). Where MergerWare could play next, three ranked bets, and a full PRD for the leading one — the "Value Engine" (synergy & value-realization intelligence).

## Notes
