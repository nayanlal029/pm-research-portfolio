## Projects

### MergerWare — M&A SaaS product research (June 2026)
Folder: [`/mergerware/`](./mergerware/) · live: https://nayanlal029.github.io/pm-research-portfolio/mergerware/

- **Competitive landscape (interactive)** — `mergerware/index.html`. A single-file map of the M&A software market: deal-lifecycle coverage across 11 vendors, AI maturity, a 2×2 positioning view, and official links to every competitor.

## Enabling the live site

After pushing, go to **Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from a branch" → Branch: `main` / `/ (root)` → Save**. The live URL resolves in about a minute.
